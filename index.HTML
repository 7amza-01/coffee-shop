About us

Welcome to our cozy coffee spot near l'École Supérieure in Guelmim! We’re more than just a café — we’re a small local project with a big love for coffee and community. Our mission is to serve quality brews in a warm, welcoming space where students, creatives, and coffee lovers can relax, connect, or get inspired. Whether you’re grabbing a quick espresso or staying to study, we’ve got you covered with great vibes and even better coffee.

Our Menu

  • Hot Beverages

    Warm up with our selection of hot drinks, carefully crafted to bring comfort and flavor in every sip. From rich espresso-based coffees to soothing teas and creamy hot chocolate, there’s something for everyone to enjoy.

  • Cold Beverages

    Beat the Guelmim heat with our refreshing cold drinks! From iced coffees and frappés to fresh juices and chilled teas, our cold beverage selection is perfect for cooling down and staying energized.

  • Refreshment

    Need a quick pick-me-up? Our refreshing options include light drinks and energizing sips to keep you feeling fresh and hydrated throughout the day — perfect for a break between classes or a chill hangout.

  • Special Combo

    Get the best of both worlds with our Special Combos! Thoughtfully paired drinks and snacks at a great price — perfect for when you're craving a little extra without the extra cost.

  • Desserts

    Treat yourself to something sweet! From classic favorites to unique delights, our dessert menu is the perfect way to end your meal — or satisfy that sugar craving anytime.

  • Burger Frenchfries

    Craving something savory? Our juicy burgers and crispy French fries are made fresh and packed with flavor — the perfect combo for a satisfying meal any time of day.
